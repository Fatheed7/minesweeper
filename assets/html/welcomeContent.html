Thanks for coming to play Minesweeper!

You can find the instructions by clicking the icon in the bottom right corner of the screen.

You can change some settings for the game by clicking the in the bottom left corner of the screen.

Please note: This site uses local storage on your device to save these settings. No confidential information is stored.

The settings stored are listed below.

  • Unrevealed Cell Colour
  • Revealed Cell Colour
  • If the welcome message should be displayed on your next visit

More information can be found on the Wikipedia article about Web Storage.

Please view the settings page if you wish to delete any local storage related to this site.
